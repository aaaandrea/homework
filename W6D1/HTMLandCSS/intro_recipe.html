Lentils!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of lentils
  • 1 chorizo (9 oz), sliced
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 medium yellow onion, diced
  • 1 potato, peeled and diced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and diced
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • olive oil
  • salt
  • optional: pickled jalepenos
  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Description

Lentejas are a traditional and hearty Spanish soup made with lentils, garlic, onion, a variety of spices and vegetables as well as chorizo.

Directions

  1. wash and soak lentils in large pot (24hrs)
  2. add water to lentil pot and heat to boil
  3. In large pan sautee chorizo, garlic, carrots, and potato.
  4. pour clean water into pan and heat to boil
  5. in separate pan fry onions until soft and slightly brown with paprika
  6. add everything into the bot with lentils and salt to taste
  7. cook for 45min
  8. serve and enjoy. (optional: add pickled jalepenos to taste)

Lentils were introduced to the Americas by Spanish and Portuguese explorers in the early 16th century. They did not gain a foothold in American cuisine, however, until World War II, when lentils were promoted as an alternative to meat, thanks to their low cost, wide availability and nutritional quality. Lentils are as good for the environment as they are for our health. Nitrogen-producing bacteria grow naturally at the roots, enriching the soil.
